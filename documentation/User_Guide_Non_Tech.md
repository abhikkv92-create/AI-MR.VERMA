The **Kernel Dispatcher** (found on the Swarm page) is where you send direct orders. Think of it as a magic terminal.

**Common Commands:**

- `/scan`: Tells the swarm to check all files for safety.
- `/heal`: Automatically fixes minor system errors.
- `/analyze [topic]`: Asks the swarm to perform a deep research dive into anything you want.

**üö® Emergency Kill**: If you ever feel the system is doing too much or you need an instant stop, click the red **"Emergency Kill"** button in the top header. This safely isolates the swarm.
